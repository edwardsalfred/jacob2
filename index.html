Pricing varies depending on the scope of your project — shoot length, location, and deliverables all play a role. Reach out for a custom quote and I’ll get back to you within 24 hours.

Absolutely! I’m available for travel both locally and out of state. Travel fees apply for locations outside of Hattiesburg, MS and will be included in your project quote upfront. No surprises.

003. How can I book? +

Booking is easy! Submit a booking request on my website, email me at Jacob@jacob1k.com, or send a DM on Instagram, TikTok, or Facebook. I recommend reaching out at least 1 week in advance to lock in your date.
